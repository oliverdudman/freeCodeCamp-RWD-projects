About us

History

We opened our first bakery in 1964 in a small town nestled in the English Countryside. We now supply custom cakes for weddings, parties and any other event you can think of.

Bespoke

All of our cakes are bespoke and are handmade to your specifications. We can cater to all sizes of event and are experts in create unique designs that will make all your guests smile.

Quality ingredients

We use only the finest ingredients and where possible, we try to get our ingredients locally. As our cakes our fresly made, they contain no unnecessary preservatives.
